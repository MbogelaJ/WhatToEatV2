Privacy Policy

NurtureNote
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Summary: NurtureNote does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All preferences are stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

NurtureNote is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Health or medical data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

2. Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Disclaimer acceptance status (session-based)
  • Pregnancy stage preference (optional)
  • Dietary considerations (optional)

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to any server. Clearing your browser data or app cache will remove this information.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. NurtureNote:

  • Does not use advertising networks
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not integrate with social media platforms
  • Does not sell or rent user data

4. No Account Required

NurtureNote does not require you to create an account or sign in. You can use all features of the app without providing any personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

NurtureNote does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is intended for adults seeking educational nutrition information.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no risk of data breach from our systems. Your preferences remain on your device only.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Educational Purpose Disclaimer

NurtureNote provides general educational reference information about nutrition during pregnancy. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for personal health concerns.
