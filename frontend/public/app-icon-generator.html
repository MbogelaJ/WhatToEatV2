📱 How to Add to Xcode:

  1. Download the 1024×1024 icon
  2. Open your Xcode project
  3. Navigate to App → Assets.xcassets → AppIcon
  4. Drag the 1024×1024 PNG into the App Store - 1x slot
  5. Xcode will auto-generate other sizes, or use an icon generator

Tip: Use appicon.co to generate all required sizes from your 1024×1024 icon.
