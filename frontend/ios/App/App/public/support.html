Frequently Asked Questions

What is NurtureNote?

NurtureNote is an educational nutrition reference app for pregnancy. It provides general information compiled from public health sources including WHO, CDC, NHS, and ACOG.

Is this medical advice?

No. NurtureNote provides general educational information only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for personal health concerns.

Where does the information come from?

Information is compiled from publicly available guidance from the World Health Organization (WHO), Centers for Disease Control (CDC), National Health Service (NHS), and American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG).

Do you collect my data?

No. NurtureNote does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All preferences are stored locally on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why do I see a disclaimer every time I open the app?

The disclaimer appears each session to ensure users understand this is educational content only. This is required for health app compliance and your safety.

I'm experiencing symptoms. What should I do?

If you are experiencing any health symptoms, please contact your healthcare provider or local emergency services immediately. This app cannot assess symptoms or provide medical guidance.
